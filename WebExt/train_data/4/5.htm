    Ingredients:

    Servings:

    Units: US | Metric

    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (or more)
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced (or more)
    • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (or other spicy mustard)
    • 12 ounces mixed greens
    • parmesan cheese, grated
    • salt and pepper

    Directions:

    1. 1
      Whisk dressing ingredients in a large bowl.
    2. 2
      Add greens.
    3. 3
      Sprinkle with parmesan and salt and pepper.
    4. 4
      Toss to coat thoroughly.
