dish name:
Lemon Dressing on Greens
from:http://www.food.com/recipe/lemon-dressing-on-greens-35574
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons lemon juice (or more)
2 cloves garlic, minced (or more)
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (or other spicy mustard)
12 ounces mixed greens
parmesan cheese, grated
salt and pepper
Directions:
1Whisk dressing ingredients in a large bowl.
2Add greens.
3Sprinkle with parmesan and salt and pepper.
4Toss to coat thoroughly.
evaluate:
A cool tangy salad for a hot summer day. Fast and easy and suitable for company.
