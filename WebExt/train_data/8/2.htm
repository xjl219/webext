Shrimp with Pineapple-Ginger Dipping Sauce

Rated by 7 people
Rate this  

More Sharing ServicesShare
Serves:
Makes 24 pieces

Spicy, sweet and tangy, the sauce would also be good with ham.

    Ingredients: 
    • 2 cups 1/2-inch cubes Whole Trade pineapple
    • 3 tablespoons 365 Everyday Value Apricot Fruit Spread
    • 3 tablespoons lime juice
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
    • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh ginger
    • 3/4 teaspoon sriracha sauce, or more to taste
    • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
    • 2 dozen Whole Catch 31/40 Peeled and Deveined, Tail-On Cooked Shrimp
    Method: 

    Put all ingredients except shrimp into a food processor and purée until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and serve with shrimp on the side for dipping.

    Nutritional Info: 
    Per Serving:15 calories (0 from fat), 0g total fat, 0g saturated fat, 10mg cholesterol, 90mg sodium, 3g carbohydrate (0g dietary fiber, 2g sugar), 1g protein
