dish name:
Sauted Green Beans
from:http://recipes.coles.com.au/recipes/1636/saut-ed-green-beans/
Ingredients:
400 g Green Beans, trimmed and cut into thirds
2 tsp Minced Garlic
4 Tomatoes, core removed and diced into cubes
1 1/2 cups Breadcrumbs
Coles Brand Extra-virgin Olive Oil Spray
Directions:
1.Place a large pan on medium-high heat and spray with olive oil.
2.Place the beans into the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring continuously.
3.Add the tomatoes and continue to cook and stir for 2-3 minutes.
4.Stir through minced garlic. Add the breadcrumbs.
