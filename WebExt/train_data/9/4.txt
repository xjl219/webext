dish name:
Mediterrannean Lamb Sandwich
from:http://recipes.coles.com.au/recipes/2534/mediterranean-lamb-sandwich/
Ingredients:
2 tbsp Reduced Fat Hummus
1 Coles Bakery Rustic Roll, split
1-2 Small Iceberg Lettuce Leaves
2 slices Tomato
2 ¨C 3 slices (about 75g) Roast Leg of Lamb, cold 
Directions:
1.Spread cut slices of Turkish bread with hummus. Top base with lettuce, tomato and lamb. Top with remaining bread. Cut in half, wrap and pack.
