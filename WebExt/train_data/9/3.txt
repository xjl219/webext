dish name:
Baked Fish on Potatoes
from:http://recipes.coles.com.au/recipes/496/baked-fish-on-potatoes/
Ingredients:
4 potatoes, scrubbed, cut into thick wedges
2 tbsp olive oil
1 small leek, thinly sliced
1 zucchini, halved, thinly sliced
1/3 cup semi dried tomatoes
4 Pink ling fillets
lemon wedges, to serve 
Directions:
1.Preheat oven to hot, 200°„C.
2.Place potatoes in a large baking dish. Toss with oil. Season to taste. Bake for 20 mins. Sprinkle leek over potatoes. Bake for a further 5 mins, until tender.
3.Stir zucchini and tomatoes through potato mixture. Place fish on top.
4.Bake for a further 10-15 mins until fish flakes when tested with a fork. Serve with lemon wedges.
