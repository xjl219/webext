Green Bean Bake

stars based on 0 reviews

For a fast dinner for the kids, adapt your sautéed green beans into a toddler-friendly green bean bake.

Cook Time 15 minutes (Prep & Cook)
Serves 4-6Serves 4-6
  • Print Recipe

Close

Tags for this recipe:

Cheese, Vegetables, Dinners, Quick & Easy, Cooking for Kids, Vegetarian, Baby & Toddler Club, Toddler , Under 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250 g Sauted Green Bean Mix, minus garlic (see recipe below)
  • 1/4 cup Breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup Coles Brand Tasty Cheese, grated

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 170C.
  2. Place the bean, tomato and breadcrumb mix in an oven dish, sprinkle evenly with breadcrumbs and cheese over the top. Place in the oven and bake for 10 minutes or until breadcrumbs have toasted and cheese melted.
