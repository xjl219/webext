dish name:
TURKEY TETRAZZINI
from:http://www.cooks.com/rec/view/0,1839,137160-230200,00.html
Ingredients:
1 lb. pasta, cooked
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms
1 stick (1/4 lb) butter
2/3 cup flour
1 large onion, sliced
5 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
4 c. chicken or turkey broth broth
2 c. light cream
1/4 cup dry sherry
4 c. diced turkey, cooked
1 c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/3 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Directions:
1.Cook and drain pasta according to package directions. Slice the mushrooms 1/8 of an inch thick and saut®¶ in 2-3 tablespoons of the butter, along with sliced onion, and chopped garlic until lightly browned. Combine the remaining butter with flour and add broth, stirring over low heat until thickened. Add the cream, salt, pepper, sherry, and garlic powder.
2.Divide the sauce in half. And one half of the turkey meat, and half of the mushrooms and cooked pasta. Butter a casserole dish and turn the pasta mixture into the casserole leaving a well in the center of the pasta.
3.Turn remaining cooked turkey and mushrooms into the center of the pasta mixture. Top with Parmesan cheese, and bake uncovered at 400°„F for 20 minutes.
Optional: may also top with buttered breadcrumbs and grated cheddar or American cheese.
