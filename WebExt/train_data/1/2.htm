Scrambled Eggs and Tomatoes

Read Reviews (16)

"A fast meal of eggs scrambled with tomatoes, and then seasoned with some salt, sugar, and soy sauce. This was the very first recipe I cooked, with a little help from my parents...I think I was about 5 or 6 at the time. Eat as it is, or serve over rice." — SCGOATS

Directions

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, scramble eggs until almost done. Remove to a plate.
  2. Return skillet to medium heat, and stir in tomatoes. Cook 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in sugar, salt, and soy. Return eggs to skillet; cook, stirring, about 1 minute more.
